Primary endpoint

GET /api/trafficmy/signals/today?limit=25

Returns line board status + today’s rider signals with glance_line summaries (time · line · place · issue).

curl -s "/api/trafficmy/signals/today?limit=5" | jq .summary,.signals[0]
Click “Try live” to fetch production data.

Use cases (B2B direction)

  • Property & HR portals — “Any delays on lines near our offices today?”
  • Newsrooms & civic tech — early rider-ground signal before operator posts
  • Logistics & field teams — commute risk for Klang Valley dispatch windows
  • Research APIs — structured today-only social transport corpus with trust metadata

Trust & limits

  • Rider signal ≠ confirmed disruption. quiet_is_not_all_clear: true always applies.
  • Raw posts and handles are never returned — synthesized summaries + source links only.
  • Write access (POST /api/refresh) requires API key in production.
  • Commercial SLA / webhooks — contact maintainer (prototype tier is free read).
