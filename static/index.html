How it works (Cara berfungsi) absence ≠ all-clear
▼

English

1. Live Rider Signals: We monitor transit mentions on Threads, Reddit, and RSS. Like a crowd-sourced road traffic app, this shows what passengers are experiencing in real time.

2. Cautious Quiet: A quiet line means no recent rider reports have been captured. It does not guarantee that service is normal. Always verify before travel.

3. Official Corroboration: We match rider signals against official announcements. Corroborated signals indicate verified service disruptions.

Bahasa Malaysia

1. Isyarat Penumpang: Kami memantau sebutan transit awam di Threads, Reddit, dan RSS. Seperti aplikasi trafik jalan raya, ini menunjukkan apa yang dialami penumpang secara langsung.

2. Senyap Bukan Sempurna: Laluan yang senyap bermakna tiada laporan penumpang direkodkan baru-baru ini. Ia tidak menjamin perkhidmatan adalah normal.

3. Pengesahan Rasmi: Laporan penumpang dipadankan dengan notis rasmi operator untuk mengesahkan gangguan perkhidmatan.
