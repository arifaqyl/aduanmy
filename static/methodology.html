Method & scope

TrafficMY is not an official RapidKL, Prasarana, or KTMB site. It aggregates recent public signals and presents cautious summaries with evidence links.

How it works

Recent public rider signals are filtered for Malaysia transport relevance, current observable impact, and duplicate reports before they become incident summaries.

Sources

  • Threads (primary rider lane when session available)
  • Reddit, news RSS, operator notices
  • GTFS for map reference and journey planning

Status rules

  • 24-hour window for line status; longer history for evidence only
  • Quiet means no qualifying recent signal, not confirmed normal service
  • Raw posts and usernames are not republished
