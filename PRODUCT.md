# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Malaysian commuters and transport watchers who check line status on their phone, often on a crowded platform or in a moving train. They need a fast, honest read of recent crowd-reported delays and official signals before they board or reroute.

## Product Purpose

TrafficMY aggregates recent public transport signals from Threads, Reddit, RSS, and official sources across Malaysia, filters noise, and presents a cautious operational summary. Success means a rider can scan line status in under five seconds and trust that quiet lines are honestly quiet, not falsely "normal."

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, station-board honest. Not a startup pitch deck. Not an alarmist news feed. Feels like infrastructure you can rely on at rush hour.

## Anti-references

- Dark SaaS dashboards with orange gradient heroes and "Know before you board" marketing copy
- Generic AI card grids with side-stripe accents and uppercase tracked section kickers
- Glassmorphism headers and decorative gradient underlines
- Pretending crowd reports are official operator status
- Cream/warm neutral backgrounds that read as 2026 AI slop

## Design Principles

1. **Task first** — the line board is the homepage, not a hero section
2. **Honest uncertainty** — "no recent reports" beats false all-clear
3. **Transit-native color** — operator line colors carry meaning; brand accent is spare
4. **Readable in daylight** — contrast and density tuned for outdoor phone use
5. **Evidence-linked** — summaries point to sources; no republished rider identities

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA contrast on body text and controls. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. High-contrast toggle for low-vision users. Touch targets ≥44px on mobile. Malay and English copy coexist naturally.
